## Design Context

### Users
Burn Rate is primarily for parents managing a single household budget. They use it on a phone to understand what is left in each household category, capture expenses quickly, and keep recurring charges or interest-free installments from surprising the month.

### Brand Personality
The interface should feel warm, home-like, clear, and practical. It should give parents calm control over household money rather than making the experience feel corporate, analytical, or overly technical.

### Aesthetic Direction
Prefer a simple, guided mobile-first flow with warm domestic cues, restrained financial precision, and plain Spanish labels. The product can be polished enough for other households to adopt, but it should still feel personal and approachable rather than SaaS-dashboard generic.

### Design Principles
- Put the next household budgeting action first: check available money, record a gasto, or review upcoming commitments.
- Keep each screen focused; reveal setup and advanced decisions only when needed.
- Use warm, grounded color and texture to make financial pressure feel manageable.
- Use plain, parent-friendly Spanish and avoid unexplained finance jargon.
- Preserve confidence through clear feedback after saves, payments, omissions, and errors.
